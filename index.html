Names Importance

A name is one of the first gifts a parent gives to their child. Names are important because they're one of the ways that we mark our place in the world. They're the first symbols of our identity and one of the first things we lead with when we meet someone new, like now

Baby Registry

There main reasons parents-to-be make a baby registry are: Gifting: to help inform friends and family that want to buy gifts which items they actually need. Planning: to keep track of all the things they need to buy before and after their baby arrives

Safety

Providing a safe environment is an important part of caring for your new baby. Babies need to be safe at home and when they’re away from home. There are some simple things you can do to help keep your baby safe and secure. Always consult your healthcare professional if you have concerns about your baby.
